Who We Are

KV Poultry Feeds Limited is a trusted manufacturer and supplier of high-quality poultry feeds. Established with a passion for excellence and sustainability, we are dedicated to improving poultry productivity and the livelihoods of farmers across Uganda. Our feeds are carefully formulated with the right balance of nutrients to promote growth, health, and egg production for both broilers and layers.

With years of experience in the poultry industry, we’ve built a reputation for reliability, consistency, and innovation — empowering farmers to achieve better results every day.

Our Mission

To provide top-quality, affordable poultry feeds that enhance growth, productivity, and sustainability for farmers across all regions.

Our Vision

To become the leading poultry feed company in East Africa, known for innovation, trust, and customer satisfaction.

Our Purpose

To empower communities through sustainable poultry farming and create lasting value for our customers and partners.
